### Understanding LYT Framework

**What is LYT?**
- LYT = Linking Your Thinking
- Created by Nick Milo
- Framework for organizing notes using Maps of Content

**Core principles:**
1. **Maps over folders:** Use MOCs instead of rigid hierarchies
2. **Heterarchical:** Notes can belong to multiple maps
3. **Emergent:** Structure emerges from connections
4. **Flexible:** Maps adapt as knowledge grows
